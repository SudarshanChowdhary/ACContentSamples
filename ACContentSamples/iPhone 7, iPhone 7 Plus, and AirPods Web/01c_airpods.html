Apple AirPods Features

AirPods are unlike any other device Apple has ever designed. Using next-generation Bluetooth technology, these wireless earbuds provide wireless freedom and stunning sound. Hover your cursor over the outlined hotspots in the interaction below to learn more about the location of hardware features.

You can set up AirPods in the same way as Powerbeats 3, Solo 3, and Beats X. Simply turn on the device, hold it within range of your iPhone, and tap the prompt to accept the connection.

You can set up AirPods with devices other than iPhone from the Bluetooth settings on the device.
