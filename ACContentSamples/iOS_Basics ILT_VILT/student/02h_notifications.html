Notifications

Notifications provide alerts and related information for iOS apps. iOS apps can provide four types of notifications:

  • Sounds: An audible alert plays.
  • Alerts: An alert dialog appears on the screen.
  • Banners: A banner appears at the top of the screen.
  • Badges: An image or number appears on the application icon.

You can view notifications in Notifications app. Access Notifications from any screen, including the Lock screen, by swiping down from the top of the screen. By default, Notifications shows Today view, which summarizes what you need to know about for the day and a peek at tomorrow.
