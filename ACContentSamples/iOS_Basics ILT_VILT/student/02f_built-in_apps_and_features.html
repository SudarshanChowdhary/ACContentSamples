Built-in Apps and Features

Built-in Apps

iOS includes built-in apps. Users can download and install thousands more using the App Store.


Built-in Features

iOS also includes several built-in features:

Apple Pay lets you pay for purchases at retail and online merchants. Apple Pay is quicker, more secure, and more convenient than paying with cash or a credit or debit card. Apple Pay requires an iPhone 6 or later and a participating merchant.


AirDrop lets you share photos, videos, websites, locations, and more with other nearby iOS devices and Mac computers.
