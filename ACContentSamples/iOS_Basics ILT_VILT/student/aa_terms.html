Key Terms and Concepts

Term/Concept Definition
AirDrop A method for quickly sharing files with other nearby iOS devices or Mac computers.
AirPlay A method for wirelessly streaming an iOS display onto another iOS device, Mac computer, or Apple TV.
Apple Pay Apple's secure method for making purchases using an iOS device with Touch ID and NFC.
barometer A device that measures changes in altitude using air pressure. iPhone 6 (or later) devices include a barometer.
NFC Near-field communications. The short-range wireless technology included in iPhone 6 (or later) devices which enables Apple Pay.
Touch ID Technology that identifies a user by placing a fingerprint against a device's Home button. You can use Touch ID to unlock a device, log into the App Store and other apps, or make a purchase with Apple Pay.
transceiver A circuit that can send and receive data. iPhone 6 (or later) devices include an NFC transceiver.
VPN Virtual Private Network. A technology for connecting to a secure network over a public Internet connection.
