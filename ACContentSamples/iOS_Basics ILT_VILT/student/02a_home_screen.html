Home Screen

All the apps that come with your iOS device—as well as the apps you download from the App Store—are on the Home screen.

Practice

On your iOS device, press the Home button to go to the Home screen and see your apps; tap an app to open it.

To return to the Home screen, press the Home button again. Swipe right to see another Home screen.

Use the following steps to customize the app layout on the Home screen, including the apps in the Dock along the bottom of the screen:

  1. Touch and hold any app on the Home screen until the icons begin to jiggle.
  2. Drag an app to move it to another spot on the Home screen, or drag it to another Home screen.
  3. Press the Home button to save your arrangement.
